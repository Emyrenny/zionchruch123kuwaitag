From the Pulpit

Greetings to all in the marvelous name of our Lord Jesus Christ.

Welcome to Zion Assemblies of God church, Kuwait. This is a full gospel Church focused on Prayers, Worship and Fellowship. If you are looking for a place to grow in the Holy Spirit by spiritual values. Here is the suitable place. We believe in the universal spiritual family redeemed through Jesus. This is a place of love, care and spiritual fellowship, focused on the word of God. We are looking for the spiritual growth of each members by giving spiritual opportunities to each one according to the grace of God provided upon us. Let us join together to worship our creator and Savior Lord and look forward to the second coming of our Lord Jesus in the mid air.

God bless you.

Pastor Shaju John
