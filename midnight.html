Mid-Night Prayer

As a spiritual team and from our experience, we have seen the tremendous impact of midnight prayers in our lives. Let’s destroy spiritual forces in prayer, as it is the hour the enemy gathers together with their charms to fight against their victims.

Luke 11:5-9 says, Jesus said to them, “Suppose you have a friend, and you go to him at midnight and say, ‘Friend, lend me three loaves of bread… I tell you, though he will not get up and give him the bread because he is his friend, yet because of the man's boldness he will get up and give him as much as he needs. "So I say to you: Ask and it will be given to you; seek and you will find; knock and the door will be opened to you.

''Let’s prayer together at midnight, hold our hands with thanksgiving, petitions and necessities of our brethren and friends."
Timings :- From 11.45 pm to 12.30 am every midnight.
Platform :- Zoom Meetings
