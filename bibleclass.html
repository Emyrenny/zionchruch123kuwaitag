Bible Class

Bible Classes in Zion AG Church is one of the most important ministry since the beginning of the church formation. We believe people discover their true purpose and identity by knowing God through His Word. Ezekiel was asked by God to take the scroll and eat it, then speak the word of God (Ezek. 2:8-3:4); as believers in Christ we need to eat the word of God, digest them, assimilate them into our being, and then speak the word of God. God’s word is the divine supply as food to nourish us, and as we pray over the word of God, God dispenses His riches into our inner being to nourish us so that we may be constituted with His element. Our normal response to the word of God should be like Jeremiah’s response in Jer. 15:16, Your words were found and I ate them, and Your word became to me the gladness and joy of my heart. Come and join our bible classes, as we need to Grow and Mature in the Life of Christ until Christ is Formed in us.
Timings - From 7.30 pm to 09.00 pm every Thursday.
