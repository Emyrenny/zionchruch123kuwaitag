About Us

In the name of our Lord Jesus Christ, it is our great pleasure and honor to welcome to the website of Zion Assemblies of God Church, Mahboula, Kuwait. We are glad for taking time to spend a few moments discovering our church family. We are a group of individuals devoted on following Jesus. We worship him with strength and joy. We encourage prayer in church and throughout life. We love people outside the church through generosity, invitation, and compassion. Our hope is that all visitors and members will feel loved and welcomed at Zion Assemblies of God Church. And once here, we pray that people will know God and as a result dedicate their lives to Jesus Christ, since we are all called to be in submission to God (James 4:7). Check us out, but more importantly, seek the truth of God that can only be known when one knows Jesus. If you’re new to church or looking for a new start, we invite you for our Thursday Evening & Friday Morning worship services, offer you the opportunity to build your faith in God, and experience in-person the presence of holy spirit through our fellowship and anointing that comes when people come together.
